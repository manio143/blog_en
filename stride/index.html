    Stride #

    I’m a contributor to the Stride game engine. It’s open source, it’s C# - I love it.

    Stride is a huge code base with over 100 projects and many users all over the world with a variety of needs. My main area of contribution looks at core libraries, serialization system, asset pipeline and also the plugin architecture.
